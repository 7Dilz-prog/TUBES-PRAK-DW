About Us

Since 2022, Scaramouchtor has been pioneering luxury, classic and custom car dealership in Indonesia, providing professional technical support to all our customers. We pride ourselves on excellent customer support - providing fully-experienced and skillful field staffs that take account of your needs and personal preferences including a warranty to carry out maintenance and repair in our exclusive partner workshops.

Scaramouchtor belongs to Five Group DW Company and start in Sentul with all of our car inventories can be viewed online. Whether it is a magnificent muscle car, a premium hatchback, or an extreme off-roader - our showroom offers a service that enables us to send your favorite car right to your garage. Simply give us a call or consult with one of our friendly staff if you have been eyeing a specific type of vehicle and let us procure the car, ship it, and deliver it anywhere in Indonesia. Our services include a consignment program that enables you to sell your car at an attractive pricing.
